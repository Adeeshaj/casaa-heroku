CASSA
Contemporary Architecture Studio and Associates

CASAA or Contemporary Architecture Studio and Associates is based out of Colombo in Sri Lanka and was established under the creative direction of Isurunath Bulankulame. Previously known as IPA Architects, founded in 2011, the organization was rebranded as CASAA in 2020.

Our award-winning practice creates bespoke spaces that are carefully tailored and personalized and commercially astute, and are designed to make the interaction meaningful to the people who encounter them.

As a design team CASAA designs structures, interiors and master plans that are admired for their refined simplicity, spatial fluidity, and thoughtful integration into their surroundings.

CASSA
Service

CASAA comprises a cross section of Architecture, Interior Design and Master Planning which is created in collaboration with a dynamic team of architects, allied professionals and technicians.

Our services are reflected through our diverse portfolio of projects spanning multiple sectors including residential, retail, hospitality, office, leisure, cultural and master planning.

We combine architecture, interiors, products and furniture to create spaces that enrich the environments in which they are situated, and lives of those who interact with them.

CASSA
Philosophy

Design and detail oriented our Philosophy is creating Contemporary architecture that is derived through strong individual concepts which are minimal, unique, site and project specific. Through creativity, collaboration and Communication with the client, we create architecture that is conducive to Client, Culture, Climate and the Context.
